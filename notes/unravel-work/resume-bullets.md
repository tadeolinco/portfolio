## Swap guidance

- Use **#6** instead of #5 for analytics-heavy roles.
- Use **#8** instead of #5 for international/APAC companies.
- Use **#9** instead of #4 for platform/devtools roles where reliability ownership reads too support-heavy.

Avoid picking 4 from one theme. A strong default set covers AI (#1), ownership (#4), business impact (#3), and core product (#5).
